How It Works

1

Create a monitor

Give it a name (e.g., "Daily Database Backup") and choose how long to wait before alerting.

2

Get your ping URL

We give you a unique URL like https://heartbeat.hermes.run/ping/a1b2c3d. Add it to your script.

3

Add it to your cron job

Append && curl -s https://heartbeat.run/ping/xxx after your command. That's the whole setup.

4

Get alerted if something breaks

If your job doesn't ping back within the grace window, we'll alert you via email, Slack, or Discord — instantly.
